About Me

Hello, Everyone.

I am Sanghyun Kim in Dyros Lab (Prof. Jaeheung Park) at Seoul National University which is working on control and planning of both humanoid and mobile manipulator. 

At 2015, I and my collegues participated in DARPA Robotics Challenge Finals 2015 (DRC Finals 2015) as Team SNU and I was studnet leader of our team. My main role was to manage whole framework of robot and developed the upper-body and lower-body position controller.

From 2018 to 2019, I have been a visiting researcher in Gepetto Team (LAAS-CNRS, Toulouse), where I have been working with Nicolas Mansard, Steve Tonneau, Pierre Fernbach, and Carlos Mastalli.

Main keywords of my reasearches are Optimal control, Trajectory Optimization, Humanoid Robot, Mobile Manipulation, and Robot Hand Control.

In this website, you can find my projects, publications, videos, and source codes. And my Curriculum Vitae (CV) is here.
